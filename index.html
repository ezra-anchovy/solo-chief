FAQ

Is my data sent to a server?

No. Solo Chief runs 100% in your browser. Your tasks are stored in localStorage. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud sync.

How is this different from Todoist / Notion?

Those are accumulators ‚Äî they help you store infinite tasks. Solo Chief is a triage engine ‚Äî it helps you decide what not to do. It scores every task on ROI and actively recommends deletions.

What's the 5-tier system?

It goes beyond the Eisenhower Matrix. T1 Critical (do now), T2 Leverage (high ROI, schedule it), T3 Interrupt (urgent but low value), T4 Distract (delete it), T5 Phantom (too vague to act on).
